## Featured Projects

**FlowSpace Product Analytics Dashboard** `Python · SQL · Streamlit · Plotly`

Built a full product analytics case study for a B2B SaaS platform (5,000 users, 174K events) covering a 6-step activation funnel, 18-month cohort retention matrix, and feature adoption framework across 13 event types. Identified a 36.3% activation bottleneck at first page creation and quantified that team invites drove 3–4x higher paid upgrade rates. Quantified a free-to-paid gap driven by 89% of free users never reaching the collaboration step. Wrote 6 BigQuery-style SQL queries using window functions and rolling aggregations; deployed an interactive Streamlit dashboard with funnel, cohort, and growth accounting views.

---

**B2B Sales Pipeline Intelligence Engine** `SQL · dbt · DuckDB · Tableau`

Analyzed 8,800 CRM opportunity records to build a deal scoring model (0–100) combining pipeline stage, deal recency, account quality, and historical win rate, tiering open pipeline into Hot/Warm/Cold. Engineered territory-normalized rep performance rankings to separate individual performance from account quality, and ran cohort win rate analysis with 3-month rolling averages segmented by rep tenure, product, and account size.

---

**Enterprise Customer Churn Prediction** `Python · XGBoost · LightGBM · SHAP · Streamlit · MLflow`

Built a churn prediction model on Telco customer data, engineering 70+ features including RFM metrics and behavioral patterns. Applied SHAP and counterfactual analysis to generate customer-specific "what-if" retention scenarios; deployed a Streamlit dashboard with real-time risk segmentation (High/Medium/Low) and a business ROI calculator projecting revenue impact of retention interventions.

---

**Credit Risk & Customer Segmentation** `SQL · Python · Power BI`

Analyzed 30K credit card transactions across 10K cardholders to segment customers by utilization, payment behavior, and delinquency risk. Isolated the top 20% highest-risk and top 15% highest-value accounts with targeted credit line adjustment recommendations across 3 risk tiers.

---

**Credit Risk & Fraud Detection** `Python · Random Forest · XGBoost · Tableau`

Processed 100K+ financial transactions and built classification models to detect high-risk applicants, achieving 93% accuracy. Designed Tableau dashboards to visualize risk exposure by customer segment.

---

**Renewable Energy Finance Analytics** `Python · Pandas · Plotly · yfinance`

Analyzed global clean energy investment and cost trends across 5 datasets (OWID, IEA, IRENA, World Bank, Yahoo Finance) covering 200+ countries and 20 years. Quantified 16x investment growth ($40B to $651B) and 88% decline in solar PV LCOE from 2010 to 2023. Built interactive visualizations including choropleth maps, cost curves, and rolling correlation heatmaps comparing clean energy ETFs against fossil fuel and S&P 500 benchmarks.
